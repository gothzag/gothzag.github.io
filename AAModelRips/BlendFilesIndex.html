Notes:

All models were pulled directly from the Dual Destinies & Spirit of Justice RomFS.
The .mods were assembled in Autodesk 3ds Max, imported into Blender as .fbxs,
then formatted and exported as .blends.

Blender is entirely free and open source. You can download Blender here.

Support Blender's development here!

File name notes:

'Wit' = Witness/out-of-court sprites
'Def' = Defense Attorneys' in-court sprites
'Pro' = Prosecutors' in-court sprites.
